WHAT IS PAYDAY?

PayDay is a web platform that allows users to easily make donations to charities. We will provide a list of charities that users can select from. PayDay will send you friendly reminders every two weeks to make a donation. These reminders will be via email or text messages based on your preference. There will also be an option to set up a recurring payment to your favorite charity.

WHY CHOOSE PAYDAY?

We make it easy for you to help the world.
You will see real data on who and how you have helped.
We will find the most needy charity for you.
Payments will be made automatically when your money comes through.
You have control over how much you donate.
